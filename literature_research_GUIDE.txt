Citation key pattern == File name of the paper
    
    Article:    [year]_[auth]_[ConferenceNameAbbrev.]
    Book:       [year]_[auth]_BOOK
    Conference: [year]_[auth]_CONF
    InBook:     [year]_[auth]_InBOOK
    InCollection:   [year]_[auth]_CONF
    InProceedings:  [year]_[auth]_CONF
    MastersThesis:  [year]_[auth]_THESIS
    PhdThesis:      [year]_[auth]_DISSERTATION
    TechReport:     [year]_[auth]_TECH_REPORT

Use the 'quality'-column to set, if inserted references are checked for correctness.

SOURCE: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11qMBE_PSBw


Research paper annotations - e.g. with OKULAR
Text marker:
- yellow: Simple importance
- green: proof/reason/key argument
- cyan: special definition          (defined in almost no previous papers)
- orange: special theorem/statement    (proofen in this paper)
